Volleyball Multi-Agent System

Game of volleyball played by autonomous agents.

  • Showcase Video

Made in python for the Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems course.

This project simulated a game of volleyball, in "abstract 3D". The simulation was written by me by hand, but the real jewel of the project are the players. They autonomously play the game, coordinating between them and performing different actions in order to win the game.
