A Child's Tale

2D Platforming game, where you play as the main characters from various famous children stories.

  • Game Page

A Child's Tale was made in Unity for the Game Development Metodology course.

This game is a 2D platformer. It includes a narrator and two bosses. I worked on both the gameplay programming, and on one of the bosses, the wolf you can reach by pressing F12. Per course requirements, there were weekly development logs. These logs detail in much more depth what each of us did, and can be found on the game's itch page.
