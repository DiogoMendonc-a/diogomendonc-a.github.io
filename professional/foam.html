Foam Sweet Foam

A dish washer simulator with a little narrative behind it.

Made for Barking Beagle Studios.

  • Itch Page (Coming Soon)

What do you do when you have a brand new team put together? You make an internal game jam to get everyone to know each other.

This game is simple: You wash dishes as events unfurl around your family that in turn affect the dishes you have to wash.

It's a relatively simple game, and so it did not require very complex programming, but it is a solid experience that I am proud of.
