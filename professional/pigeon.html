P.I.G.E.O.N

Be a pigeon, spying for the government.

Made for Barking Beagle Studios.

  • Itch Page (Coming Soon)

P.I.G.E.O.N is a short narrative RPG about being a pigeon spying for the government.

This game won the FNAC Novos Talentos 2025 award in the videogames category.

I did minimal programming for this game. This time, my role was the writer. Writing such an abstract story, from the POV of a pigeon, was a new challenge, I am proud to have had this game awarded for its writing.
