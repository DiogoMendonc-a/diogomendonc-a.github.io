Rigged Roll

Top down shooter all around throwing dice.

  • Game Page

Rigged Roll was made in Unity for the Game Maker's Tookit 2022 Jam. The theme of the jam was "Roll of of Die".

This game takes as base Vampire Survivors. The twist here is that all your bullets are a roll of a 6 sided die. Depending on which face lands up you get a different bonus. When you level up you can add a little weight to the dice to make your prefered outcome more likely.

I mostly worked on the infinite scrolling and the wave spawning function.
