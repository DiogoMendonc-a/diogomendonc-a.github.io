Inner Evil

A Visual Novel about justice and vengeance.

  • Game Page

Content warning on this one for descriptions of violence.

Sometimes it happens that a group starts working on a jam without having a programmer on the team. That was how I ended up joining a jam on the last day to implement the entire game in a hurry. It was the O2A2 jam, though, which severely restricts the scope of the game, so it was quite doable, even if it was my very first time with Ren'Py.

I so have to note, it really caught me off guard when I opened the script the team had sent me, only to find an endless enumeration of execution methods, from the "regular" to the medieval and sadistic.
